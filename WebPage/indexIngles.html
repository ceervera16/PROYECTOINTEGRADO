About Our Application

An easy way to manages a machine

CapBot Monitor is an application that provides information about the "Plastic Recycled 4.0" machine in real-time. This machine crushes plastic caps of different colors, melts them and transforms them into coils for 3D printers.

Our application offers a solution for the students who work with this machine, since it collects the data of the "Plastic Recycler 4.0" and displays them through a simple, convenient and visual interface.

Another advantage of our application is that it is easily scalable, because if in the future you need to read another field of the machine, it would not be a great effort.
